About

Hello, my name is Vitor, I am italian citizen and I've been interested in programming since childhood when I used to watch my brother and cousin coding and drawing sprites for a MSX computer (and nowadays I code and draw sprites for iOS devices). During my career I've built strong apps: from banking (Itaú and Banco Safra) to realty (Sotheby's).

After living in six different cities around the world - including São Paulo and Amsterdam - and working for many organizations, I've been able to learn a lot - on both professional and personal perspectives.

I work with mobile app development, game making and sound design. You can see some of my recent works [HERE]. I'd say I'm quite interested on audio/music, apps/videogames, IoT, Machine Learning and coffee. ☕️

I am a very optimistic person and I'm always concerned on building a good relationship with the team - also remotely.

Registered as iOS Developer since 2012, I do have domain over iOS frameworks in all iOS layers (Cocoa Touch, Media, Core Services and Core OS) and I am also registered freelancer in the Netherlands. You can find my KvK number [HERE].
