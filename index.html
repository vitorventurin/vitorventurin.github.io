Sonora by Vitor Venturin

Sonora is an app targeted to foley artists, where they can create layers of sounds, by typing words. For example, you can mix "tropical rain" + "helicopter flying" + "battlefield", and move the sound objects around your phone screen, manipulating their volume and panning. Skills: Godot framework, gdscript, free audio APIs.

Deloitte Digital

"The Do" is an internal app used by Deloitte employees, to report broken equipment from the office. It uses Vision model to predict the defective objects by image. The pilot project has showed a 40-60 percent reduction in repair time and related costs as a result of The Do app, coupled with the Smart Service Process, with more savings on the way. It is now available to all 6,500 employees across all of Deloitte’s 15 offices in the Netherlands. And coffee makers are just the beginning. The process is aimed to be rolled out for climate control systems, elevators, printers, AV-equipment, and more. Also on the horizon, the blockchain infrastructure that runs the building platform will be configured to immediately pay service companies that fix problems via smart contracts, cutting down on administrative payroll issues.
